Photo Album of Sandy Ridge Villa

This brand new villa is a 5 bedroom, 3 bathroom property with heated pool and spa. It lies just 10 minutes from Disney. The property sleeps 12 and is ideal for multiple family vacation (12 using queen sofa bed in living room). It has two master bedrooms (One with King bed & one with Queen bed) and both have en-suites and TVs. Additionally we have two queen bedrooms and a twin bedroom. It has a spacious living room with cable TV & DVD, a large dining area, and a fully fitted kitchen with breakfast table set. Outside the house there is screened, heated pool with Jacuzzi and extended decking area with child safety fence. We have carefully furnished our villa to a very high standard and designed it specifically to ensure we have provided everything you need for a wonderful holiday. The Sandy Ridge is a new community which has been developed with vacationers in mind. It is next to reunion Golf course. It is ideally located for Disney World visiting and yet still provides space and relaxation thanks to the conservation areas that surround the community.
